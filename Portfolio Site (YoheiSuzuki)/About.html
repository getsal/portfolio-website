About

My name is Yohei Suzuki, former localization manager and online marketing manager turned UX designer with professional experience of localization and online marketing. I have worked over the years as a freelancer for well known IT companies, including Google, Amazon, Intel, Booking.com, Spotify, Philips, and many more for localization projects. Also worked for an online marketing agency in Berlin, as a PPC manager. From these experiences, I have developed ananalytical approach to find small details for improvement, also project management skill to meet a tight deadline. Many years working for customer faced position and as well as marketing field, gained an ability to look from the user's perspective. After learning many valuable UX designer skill from CareerFoundry Bootcamp, such as usability analysis, user research, creating user personas and usability testing were significantly useful for my online marketing role, to bringing landing page design and conducting AB testing. Now I open the door and jump into the UX design world. I believe all of my past experiences are connected and consistent, toward end-user touchpoints. As a UX designer and researcher, I create simple design yet meeting business requirements. Feasibility is a key, observing and pay attention to the user's reaction and data to achieve a business goal. Finding the bridge between business goal and usability is not enough yet - also I find a way to connect them with language and culture differences around the world. If you are interested in my experiences, stories, and future - feel free to contact me.
