About

My name is Yohei Suzuki, former localization manager and online marketing manager turned UX designer with professional experience in localization and online marketing. My previous clients for localization projects include companies such as: Google, Amazon, Intel, Booking.com, Spotify, and Philips. Currently I am a PPC Manager for an online marketing agency in Berlin. My experience has allowed me to develop an analytical approach, identify small details for improvement, manage projects, and meet tight deadlines. Five plus years of working in customer-facing positions and in marketing have helped me understand the user's perspective. I gained valuable UX designer skills from CareerFoundry Bootcamp such as usability analysis, user research, creating user personas and usability testing. As a UX Designer and researcher I create simple designs that meet business requirements and goals. Expertise in not only bridging business goals and usability but also connecting them with language and global differences. For additional information about my experience and expertise, please contact me.
